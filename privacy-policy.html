Privacy Policy

Understanding how your data is handled when visiting Eldin Rizvanovic's official website

Overview

Our website itself does not collect or process any personal data from its visitors. Any data collection is limited to:

  • Essential hosting services provided by Netlify
  • Video player functionality provided by Vimeo

Data Collection and Processing

Website Hosting

Our website is hosted on Netlify. Netlify may collect certain technical information required for website delivery and maintenance. For details about Netlify's data practices, please refer to Netlify's Privacy Policy.

Video Content

Our website embeds videos through Vimeo's video player. When you play a video:

  • Vimeo may collect technical information about your browser and device
  • Vimeo may use cookies for player functionality
  • Vimeo may collect viewing statistics and playback information

Your Privacy Rights Under GDPR

Even though we minimize data collection, you are entitled to certain rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • The right to access information about your data
  • The right to request data correction or deletion
  • The right to restrict or object to data processing
  • The right to data portability
  • The right to withdraw consent
  • The right to file a complaint with a supervisory authority

Data Security

We prioritize the security of any information that might be collected through third-party services. Our website uses HTTPS encryption to protect data transmission.
